## Details

### homelab
- 1 site · 1 room · 1 aisle · 3 racks (all air)
- 16 compute + 2 mgmt + 1 login + 1 storage-head + 3 TOR = 23 monitored nodes
- No Slurm
- Rack: `rack-42u-air` (PDU ×2 + FAN + PSU rear)
- Checks: node_up, ipmi_temp, ipmi_power, ipmi_fan, pdu_load, pdu_current, switch_up

### small-cluster
- 1 site · 1 room · 3 aisles · 12 racks (air + DCW)
- ~120 compute (quad 2U) + 8 GPU + 2 login + 2 visu + 2 mgmt + 2 storage = 573 sim nodes
- **Slurm enabled** · partitions: cpu, gpu
- Rack: `rack-42u-air` + `rack-42u-dcw` (PMC + 2×HMC)
- Checks: + switch_port_err, ib_port_state

### hpc-cluster
- 1 site · 2 rooms · 5 aisles · 28 racks (DCW dominant)
- ~400 compute (1U 3-node DCW) + ~200 HM + ~200 GPU + services = 1841 sim nodes
- **Slurm enabled** · partitions: cpu, gpu, hm, visu
- Checks: + hmc_temp, hmc_flow, hmc_leak, pmc_power
- Metrics: node_temperature, node_power, pdu_active_power, pdu_current, pmc_power, hmc_coolant_temp

### exascale
- 3 sites (Paris, Toulouse, Berlin) · 9 rooms · 24 aisles · ~160 racks (DCW dominant)
- ~4000 compute + ~800 GPU + ~520 HM + services = 13 935 sim nodes
- **Slurm enabled** · partitions: cpu, gpu, hm, visu, bigmem, transfer
- World Map: Paris (48°N 2°E) · Toulouse (44°N 1°E) · Berlin (53°N 13°E)
